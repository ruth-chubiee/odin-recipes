Plain Okra Soup Recipe

Home

Description

One of the best soups to enjoy with seafood and "Swallow".

Ingredients

  • 15-20 Okra Fingers
  • Smoked Prawns
  • Ground Crayfish
  • Locust Beans
  • Crayfish Seasoning
  • Salt

Steps/Methods

  1. Wash and grate okro using the small size grater, otherwise, cut into chunks and put in a blender.
  2. Pour 3 cups of water in a pot, add akanwu and place over high heat.
  3. Bring to a boil, by this time it would have dissolved, Add grated okro.
  4. Pour into the pot of akanwu water. Cook until okro is tender about 5 minutes.
  5. Take off the heat and using a balloon whisk beat till everything is well mixed and okro is very mucilaginous.
  6. Put the pot back on the fire add a little salt and seasoning cube.Cook for 2-3 minutes and take off the heat.
  7. Serve Plain Okro soup, and Amala, pounded yam or any fufu of your choice.
