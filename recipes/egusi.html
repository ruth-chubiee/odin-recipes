Egusi Soup Recipe

Home

Description

This is a special Nigerian soup loved by all!.

It is usually eaten with specially prepared carbohydrates, like fufu.

Ingridents

  • Grounded Melon seeds (Egusi)
  • Stock fish
  • meat, crayfish
  • Dry Catfish
  • Palm Oil
  • Spinach leaf
  • Onion, tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Seasonings

Step

  1. Properly wah and boil the meat, dry fish and stockfish together, and season properly
  2. Chop Onions, and grind pepper
  3. Blend the melon seeds to powder and then to paste
  4. Slice the spinach leaf or any leaf of choice
  5. After preapring meat, place a new pot on fire, and pour in the red oil.
  6. Allow the oil to be hot enough for frying, pour in onions , pepper and tomatoes to fry.
  7. Stir and allow to fry well.
  8. Pour in the melon paste and allow to fry.
  9. When properly fried, add in the meat stock and the meat.
  10. Add extra seasoning if required
  11. Allow to simmer properly and add spinach leaf on top.
  12. Cook properly until fully prepared.
  13. Serve your Delicous egusi soup with fufu!
